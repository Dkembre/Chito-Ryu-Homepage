    About Us

    The United States Chito-ryu Karate Federation is comprised of Chito-ryu dojo within the United States of America, its territories and possessions. Additional schools are also located in several foreign countries. The members (instructors and students) of the United States Chito-ryu Karate Federation welcome you to our web site. The United States Chito-ryu Karate Federation is an independent non-profit organization dedicated to the scientific training, practice, improvement, and spread of Chito-ryu karate-do. If you were once a member of Chito-ryu who left and would like to once again study Chito-ryu; or if you study another style of Karate and are dissatisfied and think that you might like to become a student of Chito-ryu and a member of the United States Chito-ryu Karate Federation; or if you would like additional information about the United States Chito-ryu Karate Federation please address all inquiries to:
